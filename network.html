The Logoi Network.

Where the program takes you. The levels, the partners, the path.

What the Network Is

Logoi Digital has built a network of partner companies who are actively looking for skilled, professional remote sellers. These are not job listings you find on the internet. These are relationships Logoi has cultivated — partners who trust the quality of our training and specifically request sellers who have come through our program.

When you complete the SAP program and prove yourself — you are not left on your own. You are placed. That is the Logoi promise.

The Four Levels of Opportunity

The network is structured across four levels. Each level builds on the last. You start at Level 1 and earn your way up through performance, consistency and the completion of advanced training.

1
Level 1
Remote Sales — National
Where every Logoi seller begins.
Who it is for
Sellers who have completed the SAP program and proven themselves on Products 1, 2 and 3.
What you do
Outbound phone calls to South African service businesses. Qualify, warm up, discover pain, match solution, close.
What you need
A smartphone. A Melon SIM. A quiet space. Mobile data. Nothing else.
Commission
20% on every sale. Paid per product. No monthly targets. No deadlines. Your pace.
Placed by Logoi
Our network partners specifically request Level 1 sellers who have come through our program.
2A
Level 2 — Track A
Appointment Setter — International
For sellers ready to go global.
Who it is for
Level 1 sellers who have proven consistent performance and are ready for the international market.
What you do
Set appointments for international companies — primarily UK, US and Australia. You qualify prospects and book them into the sales pipeline.
What you need
A laptop. Reliable internet. Advanced communication training completed. English fluency is essential.
The difference
International clients require a higher standard of professionalism, faster response times and stronger objection handling. The reward is significantly higher earning potential.
How to get here
Complete Level 1 consistently. Demonstrate reliability on Slack and in your CRM. Apply when Logoi opens Level 2 placement.
2B
Level 2 — Track B
Customer Success Associate (CSA)
For sellers who want to go deeper with clients.
Who it is for
Level 1 sellers who show strong client relationship skills and want to move from closing to nurturing.
What you do
Support existing clients after setup. Handle queries, ensure satisfaction, identify upsell opportunities. The relationship does not end at the sale.
What you need
Advanced training completion. Strong communication skills. Patience, empathy and an ability to problem-solve calmly.
The difference
CSA roles focus on retention and satisfaction rather than acquisition. A different skill set — but one that builds long-term earning potential through client loyalty.
How to get here
Complete Level 1. Request CSA training. Demonstrate the empathy and follow-through the role requires.
3
Level 3 — Specialist
Medical Sales
The highest level. Specialised. Rewarding.
Who it is for
Sellers who have completed Level 1 and Level 2 and demonstrated exceptional professionalism, communication and consistency.
What you do
Sell specialised medical or healthcare products and services to clinics, practices and healthcare professionals. Consultative selling at its highest level.
What you need
Completion of Logoi's specialist medical sales training. A strong track record. Absolute professionalism in every interaction.
The difference
Medical sales clients are sophisticated buyers. They expect knowledge, precision and professionalism beyond standard sales. The earning potential at this level is significantly higher.
How to get here
This level is by invitation only. Logoi identifies sellers who demonstrate the consistency, integrity and communication standard required and invites them into specialised training.
